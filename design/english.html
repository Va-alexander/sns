About

Global Events is an international communications agency built to connect brands & people through the power of live events.

Our multi-disciplinary team is made up of fiercely creative, passionate and innovative event professionals from all corners of the globe, with vastly different backgrounds and experiences. This diversity is at the core of our creativity and inspiration. It enables us to cross-pollinate ideas, innovation, cultures and industries with best-of-breed thinking that results in creative, insight driven brand experiences that are flawlessly executed and truly connect with the audience.

Founded in 1993 in Madrid, we are privately held and have grown to become a globally recognized and award-winning full service event marketing agency. We are ranked in the top 15 International Event Agencies and service our clients from 9 offices around the globe. This growth and success can be directly attributed to not just our client services, creativity, strategy and execution; but also to our clients. It is our belief that only true partnerships, that are mutually rewarding can drive sustainable long-term growth and we are fortunate enough to work with some of the most forward thinking and innovative brands on the planet.

Now that’s enough about us…what about you?
